About

Classically trained soprano singer, graduated from the University of Edinburgh in 2020 (BMus Hons). Tania has a versatile and flexible voice with a wide-range tessitura. Latest performances include lunchtime concerts at the Reid Concert Hall (Edinburgh), Greyfriars Kirk (Edinburgh), Edinburgh Society of Musicians, and Marchmont St Giles Church Choir as a soprano scholar.

As part of her graduation recital (sadly cancelled due to COVID-19) Tania has worked closely with John Kitchen MBE (harpsichord) to deliver an interpretation of the Handel's cantata La Lucrezia. This performance will hopefully take place sometime during the summer of 2020.
